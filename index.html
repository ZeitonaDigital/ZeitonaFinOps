01 — The Challenge

The Cloud Bill Surprise.
We've all been there.

Cloud consumption is easy to start but difficult to control. Without a structured FinOps practice, costs spiral out of control, leaving finance teams frustrated and engineers restricted.

Lack of Cost Visibility

Many organizations can't attribute cloud spend to specific products or teams. Shared costs (like AKS or Storage) often remain a "black box" in the monthly invoice.

Provisioning vs. Usage

Engineers often provision for peak performance, leading to massive waste. Oversized VMs and unmanaged storage account for up to 35% of wasted cloud spend.

Commitment Complexity

Reserved Instances (RI) and Savings Plans are powerful but complex. Many companies over-commit or miss optimization opportunities because they lack the data to choose correctly.

Reactive Governance

Fixing costs after the invoice arrives is too late. Modern FinOps requires proactive policies and automated guardrails to prevent waste before it happens.
